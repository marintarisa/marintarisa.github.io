### What is a paper aeroplane?

A paper aeroplane is a simple aircraft made from a single sheet of paper. It is typically made by folding the paper into a triangular shape and then adding wings and a tail. Paper aeroplanes can be flown indoors or outdoors and can reach heights of up to 100 feet.

## Steps to Make an Amazing Paper Aeroplane

Making an amazing paper aeroplane is easy and can be done in just a few simple steps. Here's what you'll need to do:

### Gather the materials

First, you'll need to gather the materials you'll need to make your paper aeroplane. You'll need a single sheet of paper, scissors, and a ruler.

### Cut the paper into a square

Next, you'll need to cut the paper into a square. Use the ruler to make sure the edges are straight and the corners are square.

### Fold the paper into a triangle

Once the paper is cut into a square, fold it in half diagonally and crease the fold. Then, unfold the paper and fold the two sides inwards so that they meet in the middle.

### Make the wings

Now it's time to make the wings. Fold the top corners of the paper inwards and crease the folds. Then, fold the wings downwards and crease the folds.

### Make the tail

Finally, you'll need to make the tail. Fold the bottom corner of the paper upwards and crease the fold. Then, fold the tail downwards and crease the fold.
