## Definition of a Shower Head with Hose
A shower head with a hose is a shower head that is connected to a flexible hose that can be used to direct the water from the shower head wherever you want it to go. This type of shower head is often used in combination with a handheld shower head, allowing you to switch between the two for different purposes. 

## Benefits of Installing a Shower Head with Hose
Installing a shower head with a hose has many benefits. It can make cleaning your shower easier, since you can easily direct the water where you need it to go. ## Steps for Installing a Shower Head with Hose 
Installing a shower head with a hose is a relatively simple process that can be done in just a few steps. 

### Gather Necessary Tools and Materials 
Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools and materials. You’ll need a wrench, a screwdriver, a pair of pliers, a new shower head with hose, and some Teflon tape. 

### Remove Existing Shower Head 
First, use the wrench to remove the existing shower head from the wall. Once it’s removed, clean the area to make sure there’s no debris or dirt that could interfere with the installation. 

### Install the New Shower Head and Hose 
Next, use the screwdriver to attach the new shower head to the wall. Then, wrap some Teflon tape around the threads of the hose to ensure a tight seal. Finally, attach the hose to the shower head and tighten it with the pliers.
