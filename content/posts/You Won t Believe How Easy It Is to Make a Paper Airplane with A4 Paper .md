Fold the A4 paper in half lengthwise.

### Create the Wings

Fold the paper in half again, this time widthwise. Unfold the paper and fold the top corners down to the center.

### Create the Tail

Fold the bottom corners up to the center.

### Create the Body

### Make Adjustments
