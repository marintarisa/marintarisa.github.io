## What is a Dimmer Switch?

A dimmer switch is a device that is used to adjust the brightness of a light. It works by regulating the amount of electricity that is sent to the light bulb. Dimmer switches come in a variety of styles and can be used to control the brightness of both incandescent and LED lights.

## Steps for Installing a Dimmer Switch

Installing a dimmer switch is a relatively simple process. Here are the steps you should take to ensure a successful installation. 

### Gather the Necessary Materials

The first step is to gather the necessary materials. You will need a dimmer switch, a screwdriver, wire cutters, and wire nuts. You should also have a voltage tester on hand to ensure that the power is off before you begin working. 

### Turn off the Power to the Circuit

Once you have gathered the necessary materials, you will need to turn off the power to the circuit. You should do this by flipping the circuit breaker or removing the fuse from the box. 

### Remove the Old Switch

Next, you will need to remove the old switch. To do this, use a screwdriver to loosen the screws that hold the switch in place. Once the screws are loose, you can remove the switch. 

### Connect the New Dimmer Switch

Now that the old switch has been removed, you can connect the new dimmer switch. Start by connecting the black wire to the brass terminal and the white wire to the silver terminal. Then, connect the ground wire to the green terminal. Finally, secure the dimmer switch to the wall with the screws that were provided.
